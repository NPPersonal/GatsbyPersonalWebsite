## Introduction

---

**A demo simulate simple online cloth shopping store where
customers are able to navigate to different cateogry of dress. In addition customers
can add their favourite dresses to shopping cart and checkout.**

<br />

## Motivation & Purpose

---

**The purpose of creating this website is to learn [Reactjs](https://react.dev/)
framework by doing.**

<br />

## Detail

---

The website memic simple online cloth shopping store. Visitors can look into
many wears in different categories. In addition visitors can add their favourite
wear into their shopping cart and manage list of items in their cart.

Website also provide a feature to remember visitors' items in cart.
visitors can also register an account and sign webiste with the account.
Once visitors are satisfiy their shopping items then they can go to their
shopping cart and checkout.

The checkout system is in sandbox mode which mean it is not a live system.

Some frameworks are using to build this website:

- **[Reactjs](https://react.dev/)** : Core framework
- **[Redux](https://redux.js.org/)** : Manage state and shopping cart
- **[Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/)** : Account management
- **[Stripe](https://stripe.com/)** : Checkout system
- **[Expressjs](https://expressjs.com/)** : Backend server for Stripe
